Privacy Policy

MyNotes - Secure, Private, Local-First

Last updated: February 12, 2026

Quick Summary

We built MyNotes to be your private digital sanctuary. We do not sell your data. Your notes are stored locally on your device. We only request permissions that are essential for the features you choose to use (like camera for attachments or location for reminders).

1. Data Storage & Ownership

MyNotes follows a "Your Data, Your Device" philosophy:

  • Local Database: All notes, tasks, and media are stored in a cryptographically isolated SQLite database on your hardware.
  • No Cloud Sync by Default: We do not automatically upload your content to any central server.
  • Full Portability: You can export your data at any time through the "Backup & Export" settings in the app.

2. Information We Collect

Personal Data: We do not require account registration. We do not collect names, emails, or phone numbers.

Technical Usage Data: To help us fix bugs, we may receive anonymous crash reports via the store (Google Play/Apple) if you have opted into sharing diagnostic information with developers in your device settings.

3. Device Permissions

The app requests access to features to enable specific functionality:

Permission Why we need it
Location To tag notes with a location and provide geofenced reminders.
Camera To take photos of documents or memories to attach to notes.
Microphone For voice memo recording and AI speech-to-text.
Biometrics To secure the app with your fingerprint or FaceID.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following specialized SDKs which may process data according to their own policies:

  • Google Maps: Used for map visualizations (Google Maps Privacy Policy applies).
  • Google ML Kit: Used for on-device text recognition (OCR). Note: Your images never leave the device during this process.

5. Data Security

We take security seriously. MyNotes is built using the latest stable Flutter framework with ProGuard/R8 code shrinking to prevent reverse engineering. We use secure storage APIs provided by Android and iOS to manage your local data.
