Privacy Policy for MyNotes

Effective Date: February 12, 2026

Your privacy is of paramount importance to us. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information MyNotes ("we", "our", or "the app") collects and how we handle and protect your data.

1. Data Storage & Ownership

MyNotes is built as a local-first application. This means:

  • Your notes, todos, and reminders are stored primarily on your own device using a secure local database (SQLite).
  • We do not store your private notes on our servers.
  • You retain full ownership and control over your data at all times.

2. Data Collection & Usage

We do not collect or store personal identifiers like your name, email address, or phone number unless you explicitly provide them for support or feedback purposes.

Any data processed by the app (such as text extraction or image processing) is performed locally on your device whenever possible.

3. Permissions Requested

To provide enhanced productivity features, the app may request the following permissions:

  • Camera: Used to take photos and attach them directly to your notes.
  • Microphone: Used for voice-to-text input and creating audio recordings.
  • Location: Used for creating location-aware reminders and displaying map data.
  • Photo Library / Storage: Used to save attachments to your device or import existing media into your notes.
  • Biometrics: Used to provide optional app-level security (Screen Lock) using your device's native fingerprint or face recognition.

4. Third-Party Services

The app integrates with specific third-party providers to provide technical features:

  • Google Maps API: For mapping and location-based services. (Subject to Google's Privacy Policy).
  • Google ML Kit: For on-device OCR (Optical Character Recognition) and intelligent text parsing. Processing happens locally on your hardware.

5. Data Safety & Security

We implement industry-standard security practices to protect your information:

  • Encryption: Data stored on your device is protected by the host operating system's security features.
  • Obfuscation: The app's source code is obfuscated to prevent unauthorized modification or data leakage.
  • No Data Sharing: We do not sell, trade, or rent your data to third parties.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the effective date.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at our official developer portal.
